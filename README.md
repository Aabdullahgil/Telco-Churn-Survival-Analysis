## 📊 Key Findings & Visualizations

### 📉 1. Survival Probability by Contract Type
![Contract Survival](newplot%20(1).png)
**Insight:** There is a dramatic difference in retention between contract types. Two-year contracts (the top line) maintain nearly 100% survival for over 60 months, whereas month-to-month customers drop off significantly within the first year.

### ⚖️ 2. Risk Factors (Cox Hazard Ratios)
![Hazard Ratios](newplot%20(2).png)
**Insight:** This chart shows which features "push" a customer toward churning. Features like **Fiber Optic** and **Electronic Check** increase the hazard (risk), while **Online Security** and **Tech Support** act as protective factors that decrease the risk.

### 🌐 3. Internet Service Impact
![Internet Service](newplot.png)
**Insight:** Interestingly, Fiber Optic customers show a steeper decline in survival compared to DSL. This suggests that while Fiber is a premium product, it may have higher price sensitivity or service competition.
